# PulseIQ – Customer Experience Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview

PulseIQ is an end-to-end Data Analytics portfolio project designed to simulate a real-world Canadian retail business.

The project analyzes sales performance, customer feedback, returns, and support tickets to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Business Scenario

PulseStyle Retail Inc. is a fictional Canadian fashion retailer operating through:

- Online E-commerce Channel
- Physical Retail Stores

Store Locations:

- Toronto
- Mississauga
- Brampton
- Vaughan
- Etobicoke

## Key Findings

- Total Revenue: $828
- Online Revenue: $533
- Store Revenue: $295
- Order Success Rate: 80%
- Average Customer Rating: 2.8 / 5
- Product Quality Issue generated the highest refund amount.
- Negative sentiment represented 40% of customer feedback.
