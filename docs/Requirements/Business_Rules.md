# Business Rules

## Company Type

PulseStyle Retail Inc. is a fictional Canadian fashion and apparel retailer.

The company sells products through:

- Online e-commerce orders
- Physical retail stores

---

## Product Categories

PulseStyle sells:

- Dresses
- Tops
- Jackets
- Jeans
- Accessories
- Shoes

---

## Sales Channels

Orders can come from:

- Online
- Store

---

## Store Locations

Initial store locations:

- Toronto
- Mississauga
- Brampton
- Vaughan
- Etobicoke

---

## Customer Issues

Common support ticket issue types:

- Late Delivery
- Wrong Item
- Damaged Product
- Refund Delay
- Size Issue
- Payment Issue
- Poor Customer Service
- Product Not Available

---

## Return Reasons

Common return reasons:

- Wrong Size
- Damaged Item
- Changed Mind
- Late Delivery
- Product Quality Issue
- Incorrect Item Received

---

## Customer Ratings

Customer ratings will use a 1 to 5 scale.

- 1 = Very Dissatisfied
- 2 = Dissatisfied
- 3 = Neutral
- 4 = Satisfied
- 5 = Very Satisfied
