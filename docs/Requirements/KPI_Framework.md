# KPI Framework

## Customer Satisfaction KPIs

### Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

Measures overall customer satisfaction.

Target:

* Above 4.0 / 5

---

### Average Customer Rating

Measures average review rating submitted by customers.

Target:

* Above 4.2 / 5

---

## Customer Support KPIs

### Total Support Tickets

Measures total customer issues reported.

Target:

* Reduce over time

---

### Average Response Time

Measures time taken for first response.

Target:

* Under 4 hours

---

### Average Resolution Time

Measures time required to fully resolve issues.

Target:

* Under 24 hours

---

## Operations KPIs

### Return Rate

Percentage of orders returned.

Target:

* Below 8%

---

### Refund Amount

Total money refunded to customers.

Target:

* Reduce over time

---

## Business KPIs

### Revenue

Total sales revenue.

---

### Repeat Customer Rate

Percentage of customers placing multiple orders.

Target:

* Above 30%

---

### Customer Retention Rate

Percentage of customers retained over time.

Target:

* Above 80%
