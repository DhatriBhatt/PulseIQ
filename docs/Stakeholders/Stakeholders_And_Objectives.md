# Stakeholders and Business Objectives

## Primary Stakeholders

### Customer Service Manager

Responsible for customer support operations.

Questions:

* What are the most common customer complaints?
* Which issues take longest to resolve?
* How quickly are agents responding?

---

### E-Commerce Manager

Responsible for online sales and fulfillment.

Questions:

* Which products generate the most complaints?
* Which orders experience delays?
* Which regions have the highest return rates?

---

### Store Operations Manager

Responsible for physical store performance.

Questions:

* Which stores receive poor customer ratings?
* Which locations generate the most complaints?
* How does store performance vary by region?

---

### Executive Leadership Team

Responsible for business growth and profitability.

Questions:

* How satisfied are customers overall?
* What factors drive customer dissatisfaction?
* How does customer experience affect revenue?

---

## Business Objectives

### Objective 1

Improve customer satisfaction score.

---

### Objective 2

Reduce customer complaints.

---

### Objective 3

Reduce product return rates.

---

### Objective 4

Improve support response times.

---

### Objective 5

Increase customer retention.

---

### Objective 6

Generate actionable business insights using analytics and AI.
