## Analysis 1: Orders by Channel

Purpose:
Compare online versus store performance.

Results:

* Online Orders: 5
* Store Orders: 5
* Online Revenue: $533
* Store Revenue: $295

Key Insight:

Although order volume was identical across channels, online sales generated significantly more revenue than store sales.

Business Recommendation:

Management should investigate why online orders have higher average order values and consider strategies to increase store transaction values through promotions, bundles, or cross-selling initiatives.

## Analysis 2: Order Status Distribution

Purpose:
Evaluate order fulfillment performance.

Results:

* Delivered Orders: 8
* Returned Orders: 1
* Cancelled Orders: 1
* Total Orders: 10

KPI:

Order Success Rate = 80%

Key Insight:

Most orders were successfully delivered, but returns and cancellations represent opportunities for operational improvement.

Business Recommendation:

Investigate return and cancellation causes to improve customer experience and reduce revenue leakage.

## Analysis 3: Returns Analysis

Purpose:
Understand why customers return products and identify the financial impact of returns.

Results:

* Product Quality Issue: 2 returns, $128 refunded
* Wrong Size: 1 return, $89 refunded
* Changed Mind: 1 return, $59 refunded
* Damaged Item: 1 return, $29 refunded

Key Insight:

Product Quality Issues generated the highest number of returns and the highest refund cost. Wrong Size was the second most costly return reason.

Business Recommendation:

Review product quality processes and improve sizing information to reduce return rates and refund expenses.
