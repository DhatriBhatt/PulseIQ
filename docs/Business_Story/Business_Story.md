# PulseIQ Business Story

## Project Overview

PulseIQ is an AI-Powered Customer Experience Intelligence Platform designed to help businesses understand customer behavior, identify service issues, and improve customer satisfaction through data-driven decision making.

This project simulates a real-world retail and e-commerce business environment where customer interactions generate large amounts of operational and customer experience data.

The purpose of PulseIQ is to collect, analyze, and visualize customer experience metrics to uncover insights that improve business performance.

---



## Business Problem

PulseStyle Retail lacks a centralized way to understand customer experience.

Customer data exists across multiple systems including:

* Online orders
* Store purchases
* Customer support tickets
* Product returns
* Customer feedback surveys

Because data is scattered, management cannot easily identify:

* Why customers are unhappy
* Which products generate the most complaints
* Which stores perform poorly
* How support response times affect satisfaction
* How customer experience impacts revenue

---

## Project Goal

The goal of PulseIQ is to create a centralized analytics solution that transforms raw customer data into actionable business insights.

The platform will help stakeholders:

* Monitor customer satisfaction
* Identify operational issues
* Reduce customer complaints
* Improve support performance
* Increase customer retention
* Improve overall customer experience
